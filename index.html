Overview

Many of the barriers minority and immigrant family face when they are renting an apartment are the result of invisible force of unconscious bias and stereotypes. The language barriers also intensify the misunderstanding of each other.

This app is to break down those barriers and let people interact with their neighbors freely and more openly, to create a respectful digital space that supports people from different background to understand each other; to build a stronger community that minimizes crimes and raises neighborhood awareness and work together to create a better environment to live in.

The App

The login page is easy to use. You could either type in your username and password or you start a new account by clicking register.

The navigation links under the topper right menu give you a shortcut to the resources, search for keywords, and some introduction about this app and provide how to get involved option, along with the contact link.

This menu option is not exclusive for members only but visible to all people who are either already logged in or just generall checking out the app.

Experience

It only takes a few minute to register an account. The register page is simple and easy to use.

After you set up an account, you will see your profile pages and you can change your preferred language and play with other setting options.There are also rent-payment icon-directions, shortcuts to review your payment history, and quick link to other resources.

It gives you a new experience to use an neighbhorhood app. You won't believe it's that easy!

Connecting...

Before start a chat, you could pick your topic in the category page.

You choose a neighbor name who you want to contact with, and you have the option to select the chat to be private or public, depends on your suject. You can also pick Urgent or Not Urgent before startting a chat.

Communication makes things happen! Even though you don't speak the language, there's automatically translation live with your chat, directly and quickly deliver your message and solve your translation problem almost immediately in your conversation with people who don't speak your language.

Others

It also has moment page where you could view your neighbors' lately activities or you can update yours. Interaction is a choice but you could also choose to keep your life private.

It also gives your other options to take action on your building or in the neighborhood. It doesn't only provide shortcut for paying rent, but it also have linka to file complaint, solve dispute, and effectively communicate with your landlord on urgent matters.

It conveniently and simply serves your needs. It makes conversation with your neighbors and landload easy with the help of the digital technology.
